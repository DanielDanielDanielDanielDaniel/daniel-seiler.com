Project

Book a Speaker

2017

Design

Project

Branding

Opportunity

With nearly 2Bn€ annual turnover, Holtzbrinck is one of the biggest publishers in Germany and has many famous TV personalities and book authors in its portfolio.
The publisher aimed to capture a business opportunity where many event organizers and organizations would have the possibility to book famous authors and personalities.

Approach

As the head of design at Hackerbay I worked with the stakeholders to aggregate all requirements for a production ready design.
We distilled a concept into a pragmatic minimum viable product (MVP) to give a solid start that could be developed and launched quickly.

Solution

With the design language chosen, I made the speakers the biggest central visual element. The goal was to achieve an experience that sparked desire to book a personality.
The identity of the platform unifies the intellectual spectrum of the speakers. and make it attractive for speakers to be listed, but also for customers to be willing to invest.
